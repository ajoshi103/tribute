Tribute to Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was a German-born theoretical physicist known for his theory of relativity, which revolutionized the understanding of space, time, and gravity. He received the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his work on the photoelectric effect and made significant contributions to the development of the atomic bomb.

Einstein was also a strong advocate for civil rights, freedom, and pacifism. He is widely regarded as one of the most influential scientists of the 20th century.
